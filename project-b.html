Project B: A World to Explore

Welcome to my world!. The scene is currently set to load with the camera on the z axis looking straight into the negative z direction. Interact with the world with the following controls:

  1. WASD: move up, down, left, or right
  2. Q or E: move backward or forward in the direction of sight
  3. Arrow keys: pan sideways or up and down
  4. Drag anywhere within the canvas to rotate the fox and eagle.
  5. Spacebar: play/pause all animations.
