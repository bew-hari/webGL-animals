Project B: A World to Explore

Hi! I'm Bew, and to the left you can see my eagle and fox. The scene is currently set to load with the animals directly facing you. However, you can interact with them with the following controls:

  • A/D: move sideways, perpendicular to direction of sight
  • W/S: move up or down, in absolute z coordinates
  • Q/E: move backward or forward in the direction of sight
  • Left/right arrow: pan sideways
  • Up/down arrow: pan up or down
  • Spacebar: play/pause all animations.
